What is Dental Bonding?

Dental bonding is a procedure where a tooth-colored resin material is applied to the teeth and hardened with a special light. This resin bonds directly to the tooth, effectively improving its shape, color, and strength. Patients often choose bonding to fix:

  • Cracked or chipped teeth
  • Discolored or stained enamel
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Misshapen or uneven teeth
  • Teeth that appear shorter than normal
